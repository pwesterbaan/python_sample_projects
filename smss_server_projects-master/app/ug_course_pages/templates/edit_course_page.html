2. Edit Course Page
Instructions:
  • Add an item by clicking the button below the category title. If a category is left blank, it will not display on your course page.
  • Delete an item by clicking the button on the far right side of the toolbar.
  • Insert a link to uploaded content by clicking the button and selecting from the link list dropdown at the bottom of the popup.
  • Save your changes by clicking the Save Changes button at the bottom of this page. Your changes are not saved until you click this button.
  • The text editors behave similar to Microsoft Word or any other document editing software. If you hover your mouse over the buttons on the toolbar, a tooltip will display describing the action of the button. For most of the buttons you will need to highlight some text and then click the button to apply the effect to the selected text.
